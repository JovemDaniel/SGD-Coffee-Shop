About Us

Coffe Soli Deo Gloria is a coffee shop created with the aim of helping the Soli Deo Gloria Project, all the profit we make goes directly to help maintain the project and its mission, to create bivocational young people. Students learn theology and technology for a year, and may be employed in Recife or in neighboring countries so that they can spread the Reformed faith.

Did you like the initiative? Come help us continue it, try Cafe Soli Deo Gloria.
